About CAPITOL RESIDENCY

Capitol Residency is a first class business,full service hotel in Ranchi , very well located near the Airport,the Railway station and the central business district of Ranchi. Exquisite interiors, impeccable service and fine cuisine combine to make this the Ranchi's most graceful and elegant hotel. The hotel is ideal for business travel or holidays to explore the cultural wonders of Ranchi.
